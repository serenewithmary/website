about ✦

How I got
here.

I crossed the Pacific at twelve. I learned English in middle school cafeterias and learned my own mind through therapy rooms, twelve-step rooms, and EMDR sessions. The diagnoses are real — OCD, anxiety, depression, bulimia, PTSD. So is the recovery.

Art is what saved me. Photoshop, in particular, around age 23. Writing, around the same time. Music has always been there. So has the slow practice of sitting with what hurts and not running.

This site is not a clinic. I'm not a therapist. I'm someone who has spent enough years inside several kinds of rooms to believe that the survivor's voice matters too — and that wisdom, when shared honestly, becomes a kind of medicine that travels.
